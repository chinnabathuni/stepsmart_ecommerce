Online Shopping Mart

An online shopping mart, also known as an online marketplace or e-commerce platform, is a virtual store where customers can browse and purchase products or services over the internet. These platforms often offer a wide range of products from various sellers, providing customers with convenience, choice, and often competitive pricing. Users can typically create accounts, add items to a virtual shopping cart, and securely complete transactions using various payment methods. Online shopping marts may also feature reviews, ratings, and recommendations to help customers make informed purchasing decisions.
